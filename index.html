What Should I Cook?

This app helps hungry users easily decide what to cook without overwhelming them with options. If they can't choose what to cook from a limited set of recipes, they are returned restaurants nearby or can choose to see more recipes.

I wrote it because most recipe apps have endless scrolling, which is nice for exploring new recipes but doesn't always help when you're hangry and need to decide on what to cook ASAP.

Technologies Used: jQuery, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
