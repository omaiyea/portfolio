Basic Science Quiz

This quiz tests users on basic science concepts and gives background into any concepts they may not remember. It's for anyone who's taken even just one intro-level science class.

Technologies Used: jQuery, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Why: I made it to help remind people of some of the common concepts you see in every day life. Science literacy is important, and approachable quizzes may be one tiny step towards that!

Wireframes: Normally I wireframe in Balsamiq, but for this project, I put together live HTML wireframes.

Future Directions: From user testing, for most this quiz was probably too easy. In the future, I'd like to present progressively harder questions, if the user is acing the easier ones, to challenge those with more background.

What Should I Cook?

Hungry users can use this app to decide what to eat. If they can't decide what to cook from a limited set of personalized recipes, they're shown restaurants nearby to eat out at instead.

Technologies Used: jQuery, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Research: Most recipe apps have endless scrolling, which is nice for exploring new recipes, but doesn't always help users who are hungry and need to decide on what to cook ASAP. Limiting options may be a way to get to a decision sooner.

Wireframes: I also put together live HTML wireframes. for this project.

Future: Testing... it's on the list! Especially because this app works with several differen APIs. Even during development, sometimes the APIs would change and unexpected data would be returned.
